Who We Are

Dept. of Metallurgical & Materials Engineering

Established in 2013, the Department of Metallurgical & Materials Engineering at VSSUT Burla is a unique initiative in India's science and technology education landscape — devoted to the design, creation, and fundamental understanding of materials that enhance the human experience.

Our 4-year undergraduate programme provides deep technical knowledge across Physical Metallurgy, Extractive Metallurgy, and Mechanical Metallurgy — from manufacture and testing of metals and alloys to their deformation and failure mechanisms, materials characterisation, and structure–property relationships.

Our Vision
What Dravya Stands For
Dravya stands as the prestigious annual festival of the Metallurgical and Materials Engineering Department — a grand celebration of intellect, innovation, and fellowship. This remarkable event unites students, faculty, and industry leaders, crafting a vibrant platform for showcasing technical prowess, cultural artistry, and stimulating dialogues.

In the last edition, we proudly commemorated a decade of excellence, reconnecting with alumni who shared their valuable experiences and insights, inspiring future generations. The festival brims with exhilarating competitions, thought-provoking lectures, and delightful activities that encourage participants to shine — nurturing an atmosphere of learning, collaboration, and celebration, ensuring an unforgettable experience for all who partake.
